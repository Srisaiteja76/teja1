Buying subscriptions for ott applications and many other applications have now become common among the younger generations and even more common among students. Students have a larger appetite for subscription-based apps and generally, it is more significant than their pockets. Applications whose revenue stream is dependent on the subscription model always try to offer a free period to get the customers habituated to their product. This works for many but for students, the price becomes a barrier.

OBJECTIVE OF OUR PROJECT

Project description

our website help brands acquire customers who like their product. Companies prefer this model of price discrimination because students are the most sensitive to prices. Another reason why companies differ their prices for students is customer loyalty. Yet another perk of earning college students’ loyalty is that they’re natural brand ambassadors. If they like something, they share it with everyone they know. This strategy also helped apple in overtaking dell as the most popular brand in the student laptop segment.our project helps the service providers to overcome the problem of fake profiles. They can identify who must be given discounts and who must not be given.
